How to Make Money Writing Online

Making money by writing online is a real way to earn extra income or even build a full-time career. This guide shows you simple ways to start earning money with your writing skills, even if you're just beginning.

What You'll Need to Start

Before you begin, make sure you have:

  • A computer or tablet with internet access
  • Basic writing skills
  • A quiet place to work
  • Some time each week to write
  • A PayPal account or other way to receive payments
Writing Setup Image

Step 1: Find Your Writing Niche

Think about what you know well or enjoy writing about. This could be:

  • Technology products
  • Health and wellness
  • Personal finance
  • Travel experiences
  • Food and cooking

Choosing a specific topic area helps you find the right opportunities.

Step 1 Image

Step 2: Create Writing Samples

Before you can get paid, you need to show people your writing. Create 2-3 sample articles about your chosen topics.

These don't need to be published anywhere - just well-written pieces that show your style and knowledge.

Keep them between 500-800 words each, and make sure they're free of spelling errors.

Step 2 Image

Step 3: Find Writing Opportunities

Look for websites and companies that pay writers. Good places to start include:

  • Content mills like Textbroker or iWriter
  • Freelance platforms like Upwork or Fiverr
  • Blogs that accept guest posts
  • Local businesses that might need website content

Apply to several places to increase your chances.

Step 3 Image

Step 4: Set Your Rates

Decide how much you want to charge for your writing. Beginners might start with:

  • $0.03-$0.05 per word for articles
  • $15-$25 per hour for general writing
  • $50-$100 for blog posts

As you gain experience, you can gradually increase your rates.

Step 4 Image

Step 5: Deliver Quality Work

When you get a writing job, make sure to:

  • Follow the client's instructions carefully
  • Submit your work on time
  • Proofread for errors before sending
  • Be open to feedback and revisions

Good work leads to repeat business and referrals.

Step 5 Image

Helpful Tips for Success

  • Start with smaller jobs to build your portfolio
  • Specialize in a few topics rather than trying to write about everything
  • Set up a simple website or blog to showcase your writing
  • Join online writing communities to learn from others
  • Be patient - it takes time to build a steady writing income
  • Always meet your deadlines to build a good reputation
